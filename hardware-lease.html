Choosing the Right Hardware

In today's interconnected world, having a reliable experience can make all the difference in staying connected wherever you go. We offer two great options, each catering to different needs and preferences. Let’s take a closer look at the MR1100 and the LBR20, highlighting their key features.

MR1100: Mobility at its Best

The MR1100 is designed for mobile use, providing reliable LTE internet wherever you go. Its compact and portable design makes it an ideal choice for travelers or anyone needing internet on the move. Whether you’re in an RV, camper, or working remotely, the MR1100 offers seamless connectivity in areas where traditional Wi-Fi is unavailable.

Pro-Tip: Many users pair the MR1100 with their home router to extend coverage, making it a solution for both on-the-go and home internet needs.

LBR20: Perfect for Fixed Locations

The LBR20 is built for fixed use, designed to deliver fast and reliable LTE internet at home or in small offices. It supports multiple devices simultaneously, ensuring a stable connection even in busy households. Ideal for those who need a consistent, always-on connection.

MR1100 vs LBR20: Which is Right for You?

The MR1100 excels at providing mobile connectivity, offering flexibility for those who are always on the go, while the LBR20 is built for fixed locations, making it ideal for stable home or office setups. Consider your primary usage: if you need internet while traveling or in areas where fixed Wi-Fi isn’t available, the MR1100 is your best choice. However, if you’re looking for a reliable internet solution at home or in a small office, the LBR20 is a perfect fit.

Conclusion

Whether you choose the MR1100 for mobile use or the LBR20 for fixed locations, both options offer excellent performance and reliability. Assess your needs and lifestyle to determine which device will keep you connected, no matter where you are.
