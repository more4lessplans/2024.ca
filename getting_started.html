Setup guide

01
Important — do not factory reset your device
⚠
Warning: Do Not Factory Reset

Never perform a Factory Reset using the pinhole button or software settings. Your device has been pre-configured with a custom APN that enables your unlimited data plan. Resetting will permanently erase these settings and disconnect your service.

02
Battery & hardware

The battery is already installed for your convenience. To access it, firmly slide the back cover upward to unlatch it from the device frame. Ensure the battery is seated firmly against the gold contact pins, then slide the cover back down until it clicks. We recommend charging the unit fully before first use.

03
Power on

Press and hold the Power button on the top or front of the unit for 3 seconds until the screen illuminates. Allow roughly 60 seconds for the internal modem to fully initialise and connect.

04
Placement tip

For the strongest signal, place the hotspot near a window or on an upper floor. Avoid metal enclosures, thick concrete walls, and large appliances.

05
Personalise your Wi-Fi

To change your Wi-Fi network name or password, or to check real-time signal strength and data usage, download the Nighthawk management app.

01
Power connection

This unit must remain plugged in at all times. Connect the provided 12V power adapter to the Power port on the rear of the Orbi and plug it into a wall outlet. Ensure the On/Off button on the back is pushed in to the on position.

02
Placement tip

For the best possible performance, place the Orbi near a window or on an upper floor. Avoid placing it inside metal cabinets, near large appliances, or in basement areas.

03
Connect your devices

Your Wi-Fi network name and password are printed on the label at the base of the router. Connect any device — phone, laptop, tablet — using those credentials.

04
Personalise your Wi-Fi
