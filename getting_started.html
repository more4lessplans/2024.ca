Step 1

Hardware & Power

01
Do Not Factory Reset Your Device
⚠
Critical Warning

Your Nighthawk has been specially configured to work with your unlimited data plan. Never perform a factory reset using the reset pinhole or the device settings menu. A factory reset will erase these custom settings and immediately disconnect your service.

02
The Battery

The battery is pre-installed for your convenience. To access it, slide the back cover upward until it detaches from the device. Ensure the battery is firmly seated against the gold contacts, then replace the cover until it clicks securely into place. For optimal performance, fully charge the device before first use.

Note: If you plan to use the device plugged in continuously (24/7), we recommend removing the battery.

03
Power On

Press and hold the Power button for approximately 3 seconds until the display turns on. Allow up to 60 seconds for the modem to connect to the network.

04
Choose The Best Location

For the strongest signal and fastest speeds, place the hotspot near a window or on an upper floor. Avoid metal cabinets, thick concrete walls, and large appliances that may interfere with cellular signal reception.

01
Connect Power

Connect the included power adapter to the power port on the rear of the Orbi and plug it into a wall outlet. Ensure the rear power switch is turned on. The LED ring will illuminate while the router starts up.

02
Choose The Best Location

For optimal performance, place the Orbi near a window or on an upper floor. Avoid basements, metal enclosures, and locations near large appliances that can weaken the cellular signal.

03
Connect Your Devices

Your Wi-Fi network name and password are printed on the label on the bottom of the router. Connect your phones, tablets, computers, and smart devices using these credentials. The Orbi supports up to 64 connected devices.
