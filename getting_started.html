Hardware Preparation

Referencing Netgear Nighthawk Official User Instructions:

Removable Battery

The battery is already installed for your convenience. To access it, firmly slide the back cover upward to unlatch it from the device frame. Once open, ensure the battery is seated firmly against the gold contact pins. Slide the cover back down until it clicks into place. We recommend charging the unit fully before the first use.

Powering On

Press and hold the Power Button on the top or front of the unit for 3 seconds until the screen illuminates. Wait roughly 60 seconds for the internal modem to initialize.

3

Connecting to WiFi

Your default network credentials can be found in two places:

  • On Device: Press the power button quickly to cycle through the LCD screens until "WiFi Name" and "WiFi Password" appear.
  • Behind Battery: Remove the back cover and battery; the default SSID and Password are printed on the internal label.
Warning: Do Not Reset

NEVER perform a Factory Reset using the pinhole or software settings. Your device has been pre-configured with a custom APN to allow unlimited data access. Resetting will erase these settings and disconnect your service.

2

Hardware Preparation

Referencing Netgear Orbi LBR20 Official User Instructions:

Power Connection

This unit must remain plugged in. Connect the provided 12V power adapter to the "Power" port on the back of the Orbi and plug it into a wall outlet. Ensure the On/Off button on the back is pushed in.

Placement Tip

For the best possible high-speed performance, place the Orbi near a window or on an upper floor. Avoid placing it inside metal cabinets or near large appliances.

3

Connecting to WiFi

Retrieve your unique network details:

Look at the Bottom of the Device. You will see a white label containing your unique Network Name (SSID) and Network Key (Password). Connect your phone or computer to this network to get online.

Warning: Do Not Reset

DO NOT PRESS THE RESET BUTTON. This device is professionally configured for our specific network. A factory reset will wipe the APN settings, requiring the unit to be sent back for re-programming.
