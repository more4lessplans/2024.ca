Choosing the Right Hardware

In today's interconnected world, having a reliable experience can make all the difference in staying connected wherever you go. We offer several impressive options, each catering to different needs and preferences. Let’s take a closer look at the options, to help you decide which one suits you best.

MR1100: Mobility at an Affordable Price

The MR1100 is a standout for its affordability and robust mobility features. It supports LTE connections and is designed to deliver fast internet speeds on the go. With its sleek, portable design, the MR1100 is perfect for travelers or anyone who needs reliable internet outside of traditional Wi-Fi networks.

**Pro-Tip:** Many users opt to pair the MR1100 with their existing home router to extend their internet connection throughout their entire household, making it a versatile choice for both mobility and home use.

LBR20: Tried and True Reliability

Priced mid-range, the LBR20 is known for its reliability and performance. It provides more than enough speed and capability for most users' needs, making it an excellent choice for homes or small offices. The LBR20 supports LTE connections and is designed to handle multiple devices simultaneously without compromising on speed.

NBR70: Harnessing the Power of 5G

At the top end, the NBR70 stands out with its advanced 5G capabilities. Designed to take advantage of the latest in mobile network technology, the NBR70 promises blazing-fast speeds and low latency, making it ideal for heavy-duty applications such as 4K video streaming, virtual reality, or online gaming.

While it comes at a higher price point, the NBR70 is future-proofed to support upcoming advancements in mobile connectivity, ensuring that you stay ahead of the curve in terms of internet speed and reliability.

Making the Choice: What Hardware is Right for You?

When deciding on your hardware, consider your specific needs for speed, reliability, and future-proofing. Whether you opt for the affordability and mobility of the MR1100, the tried and true reliability of the LBR20, or the advanced 5G capabilities of the NBR70, there is a solution that meets your requirements. Make sure to assess your usage patterns and budget to select the router that best fits your lifestyle and internet demands.

Conclusion

With any of these devices, you can rest assured that you’re investing in quality, performance, and connectivity that keeps you seamlessly connected, whether you’re at home, at work, or on the move.
