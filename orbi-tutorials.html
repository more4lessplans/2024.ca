Fixed Device Support

Step-by-step guides for your Netgear Orbi router.

Update the APN on your Orbi router for optimal connectivity.

  1. Open the Orbi App: Launch the NETGEAR Orbi app on your smartphone.
  2. Connect to Wi-Fi: Ensure your device is connected to your Orbi's network.
  3. Router Admin Login: When prompted, use username admin and password more4less (or More4LessPlans).
  4. Go to Router Settings: Tap the menu icon and navigate to Settings > Advanced > Mobile > APN.
  5. Add a New APN: Tap Add and enter the APN provided in your activation email.
  6. Save & Restart: Tap Save or Apply and reboot your router.

Follow these steps to manually select a carrier if your signal is unstable.

  1. Open the Orbi App: Ensure you are logged in and connected to the router.
  2. Router Admin Login: Use admin and more4less (or More4LessPlans).
  3. Access Mobile Settings: Navigate to Settings > Advanced > Mobile.
  4. Network Selection: Tap Network Selection and switch from Auto to Manual.
  5. Choose Carrier: Select your preferred carrier (e.g., Rogers, Bell, or Telus) from the list.
  6. Apply & Restart: Save your settings and reboot the Orbi.
