Nighthawk Support

Follow these steps to configure your device for optimal performance.

Follow these steps to update the APN on your Nighthawk for reliable connectivity.

  1. Download the NETGEAR Mobile App: If you haven't already, download and install the NETGEAR Mobile app from the App Store (iOS) or Google Play Store (Android).
  2. Open the App: Launch the NETGEAR Mobile app on your smartphone or tablet.
  3. Log in with NETGEAR Account: Log in with your NETGEAR account credentials or create an account if you don't have one.
  4. Connect to Your Router: Make sure your device is connected to the Nighthawk's network.
  5. Router Admin Login: Log in with username admin and password attadmin.
  6. Go to Router Settings: Tap the menu icon or settings icon to access router settings.
  7. Access Mobile Settings: Navigate to Settings > Advanced > Mobile > APN.
  8. Add a New APN: Tap Add to create a new APN profile.
  9. Enter APN Details: Type the required APN (contact support for this) and leave other fields blank.
  10. Save the Settings: Tap Save or Apply.
  11. Restart the Router: Reboot your Netgear Nighthawk router.
  12. Verify Connection: Check your internet connection after rebooting.

Follow these steps to manually select a network on your Nighthawk for better control.

  1. Download the NETGEAR Mobile App: Download and install from the App Store or Google Play Store.
  2. Open the App: Launch the NETGEAR Mobile app.
  3. Log in with NETGEAR Account: Use your NETGEAR credentials or create an account.
  4. Connect to Your Router: Ensure connection to the Nighthawk's network.
  5. Router Admin Login: Use admin and attadmin.
  6. Go to Router Settings: Access settings via the menu or settings icon.
  7. Access Mobile Settings: Go to Settings > Advanced > Mobile.
  8. Select Manual Network Selection: Tap Network Selection.
  9. Choose Manual: Switch from Auto to Manual.
  10. Select Your Preferred Carrier: Choose from the list of available carriers.
  11. Save the Settings: Tap Save or Apply.
  12. Restart the Router: Reboot your Netgear Nighthawk router.
  13. Verify Connection: Confirm the connection works with your selected carrier.

Follow these steps to lock specific LTE bands on your Nighthawk for optimized performance.

  1. Download the NETGEAR Mobile App: Install from the App Store or Google Play Store.
  2. Open the App: Launch the NETGEAR Mobile app.
  3. Log in with NETGEAR Account: Log in or create an account.
  4. Connect to Your Router: Connect to the routers network.
  5. Router Admin Login: Use admin and attadmin.
  6. Go to Router Settings: Access via menu or settings icon.
  7. Access Mobile Settings: Navigate to Settings > Advanced > Mobile.
  8. Select LTE Bands: Tap LTE Bands.
  9. Choose Preferred LTE Band: Select from the list (e.g., Band 2, Band 4, Band 12).
  10. Save the Settings: Tap Save or Apply.
  11. Restart the Router: Reboot your Netgear router.
  12. Verify Connection: Check the connection with your selected LTE band.
